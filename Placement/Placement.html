Career Developement Center

JIMS Greater Noida has redesigned its Training and Placement department into Career Development Centre (CDC) that provides career solutions to the students. We started with the mission to 'Make every student of JIMS a Career Oriented person instead of a Job Oriented Person'. It is continuously engaged in building relationships with corporate sectors and building careers of the students.

JIMS, Greater Noida is well known as best placement college in Delhi/NCR for placing students in leading companies. The placement team organized various career development programs throughout the year for our students-

  • Improve soft skills, professional communication and personality through career counseling
  • Help prepare professional resumes by conducting resume writing workshops
  • Each year our placement process starts with characteristic zeal and gusto. Our enterprising and proactive placement cell, which is engaged in organizing seminars, guest lectures, conferences, industrial visits, summer training and other corporate events throughout the year, begins by inviting top corporate for final placement from September onwards.
