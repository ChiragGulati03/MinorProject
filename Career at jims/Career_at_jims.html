CAREER AT JIMS

For more than 10 years, JIMS College has dedicated itself to the educational needs of society. The involvement of our employees has been key to our reputation as a leader in educating students and in the development of our unique culture.

If you would like to become a partner in our community of learning, please see our list of open positions below. Please note that applications and resumes are accepted only when there is an open position, except for faculty positions, which will be accepted at all times. All applicants should specify the position for which they are applying. Unsolicited resumes will not be retained or considered except for faculty positions. Applications and resumes are considered active for 60 days.

Status of Your Employment Appliaction

Resumes and applications received in Human Resources are forwarded to the hiring department. Department managers arrange their own interviews. If you have not been contacted for an interview and the position is filled, you are encouraged to apply for other open positions at JIMS for which you may be qualified.

Employee Benifits

    Eligibility for benefits is based upon your employment status at the College. These benefits include:

  • Medical, dental and vision coverage
  • Life and disability insurance
  • Flexible spending accounts for health and dependent care
  • Retirement plan with college match
  • Employee assistance program
  • Tuition remission for employees
  • Tuition program for dependents
  • Paid vacation, holidays and sick time
