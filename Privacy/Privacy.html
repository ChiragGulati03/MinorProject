PRIVACY POLICY

Jims College Website Privacy Policy

The purpose of this policy is to protect the privacy of individuals who have sensitive information stored electronically on asssts owned by JIMS College, while at the same time providing the College the ability to share this information with authorized entities as required by legitimate academic or business need or by law. This policy is not intended to replace or supersede other existing College policies and procedures relationg to the use and maintenance of sensitive information such as those related to FERPA compliance.

Section 1: Privacy Statement

JIMS is required by state and federal laws to keep certain information confidence. Privacy and confidentiality is balanced with the need for the college to manage and maintain its networks, keep all members of our community safe, and provide the services requested by our employees, students and their families.

Section 2: Kinds of Information Collected

You may choose how much personally identifiable information to provide to JIMS, Information collected online is often defined as being either personally identifiable or anonymous:

Personally Identified information tells JIMS who is using the website and includes information you may requested that was we store on the website for your future. JIMS may need this information to provide a personalized or enchanged services that you requested- such as additional information or online payments. While we hope that you will find it benefical to judiciously provide Personally Identifiable Information to JIMS so that we can provide the best practical service to you, the amount of Personally Identifiable Information that you choose to disclose is a decision you make.
Anonymous Profile Information refers to information that cannot easily be tied back to a specific individual. For instance, we may know that 2,000 people visited this Privacy Policy page today, but we do not necessarily know their names or how to contact them.

Section 3: Family Education Rights & Privacy Act (FERPA)

In addition to the rights conveyed to you by this Privacy Policy, the Federal Family Educational Rights & Privacy Act (FERPA) affords students certain rights with respect to their education records. These rights include:

  • The right to inspect and review the student’s education records within 45 days of the day the College receives a request for access;
  • The right to request the amendment of the student’s education records that the student believes are inaccurate, misleading or otherwise in violation of the student’s privacy rights under FERPA;
  • The right to provide written consent before the College discloses personally identifiable information from the student’s education records, except to the extent that FERPA authorizes disclosure without consent;
  • The right to restrict JIMS’s use of student’s directory information, which otherwise will be shared freely by the College; and
  • The right to file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Education concerning alleged failures by JIMS to comply with the requirements of FERPA.

Section 4: Privacy Policy Updates

JIMS reserves the right to amend this policy at any time, for any reason, without notice to you. Any changes will be published here and you should check our website to access our current Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy was last updated on May 15, 2019, and was approved by the Technology Steering Committee on May 16, 2019.

Section 5: Consent

By using this website, you agree to all of the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any term of this Privacy Policy, please do not submit any Personally Identifiable Information.
