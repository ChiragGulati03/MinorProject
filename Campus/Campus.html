THERS'S MORE TO LIFE A CLASS

You’re not just choosing a school — you’re choosing a home. On JIMS’s beautiful campus you’ll find a diverse, welcoming
community of students and faculty, plus opportunities to learn, lead, explore and give back, not to mention have fun. And you don’t
have to live on campus to enjoy a host of activities and special events (but spoiler: you may not want to leave once you’re here).

Residencial Life

From late-night study sessions to lifelong friends, our residence
halls will give you so much more than just a place to sleep. We
offer room and meal plan options for every budget. Ready to move in?

Students Activities & Leadership

Choose your own adventure! With 30+ student organizations
and numerous leadership opportunities, it’s easy to find your fit
(and enhance your resume). Where will you start?

Campus Ministry

Interested in exploring your spiritual side and serving your commmunity? we offer inclusive
programming for student from a wide range of faith traditions as well as voluntee opportunities in
Milwaukee and beyond.

Living in Milwaukee

Gorgeous Lake Michigan, an amazing arts and entertainment scene, delicious dining, and nonstop
summer festivals- there's so much to discover in Wisconsin's largest city, and JIMS puts you
close to the action.

Campus Safety

Your safety is our No.1 priority. Learn more about our safety and security resources and how we
maintain JIMS safe campus environment.

Athletics

Welcome to the home of the inferno! Join or cheer one of our seven NCAA Division III sports:
basketball, cross country, golf, soccer, softball, tennis and vollyball.

Wellness

We're here to help you care for your mental and physical health. For a healthy lifestyle, we offer an
on-campus nurse, counselling office and fitness center.

A-Store

Visit the A-store near the Commons for JIMS gear and more! Or shop online. Show them
you're JIMS Strong!
